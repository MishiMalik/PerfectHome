ABOUT ME

Tyler Antrican

I have a passion for real estate and I'm on a mission to deliver the highest quality of service to my clients in Lake Norman and the Greater Charlotte Area. Unfortunately, I had suboptimal experiences when I was purchasing my own homes. My agents were all knowledgeable, but they treated me simply as a client. I didn't feel like my agent was listening or providing me what I needed to make this a successful and positive experience. My experiences have shaped my approach to the real estate journey, and I want to ensure that the trust my clients placed in me is reciprocated by my acute ability to be a real estate matchmaker. Communication is key to the success of a real estate transaction, so you can trust that I'll always keep the lines of communication open.
